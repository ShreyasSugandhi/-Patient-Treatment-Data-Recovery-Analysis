🏥 **Healthcare Analytics Dashboard | Power BI Project**

This project presents a comprehensive Healthcare Data Insights & Performance Dashboard built using Power BI. It analyzes patient data across multiple departments to uncover key insights related to treatment costs, recovery outcomes, hospital performance, and revenue generation.

The dashboard covers 200 patient records and provides a complete view of healthcare operations through interactive visuals and KPIs.

📊 **Key Highlights**
📌 Patient Metrics: Total Patients, Average Age, Recovery Score, Hospital Stay
💰 Financial Analysis: Treatment Cost, Revenue by Department, Revenue per Day
🏥 Department Insights: Performance comparison across Cardiology, Orthopedics, Neurology, etc.
💉 Treatment Analysis: Cost vs Recovery Score across Medication, Surgery, Therapy, Observation
👨‍⚕️ Doctor Performance: Recovery score benchmarking across doctors
👥 Demographics: Patient distribution by age group and gender

**📈 Key Insights**
Surgery is the highest cost (~$83K) but delivers the best recovery outcomes (~73%)
Orthopedics generates the highest revenue (~$3.4M)
Observation & Therapy are cost-efficient but slightly lower in recovery performance
Top doctors consistently achieve >72 recovery scores
Average hospital stay is 16 days, indicating optimization opportunities
